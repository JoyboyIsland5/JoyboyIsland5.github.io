A new journey begins as we set sail into the open sea.

The winds are favorable, and the crew is ready. The horizon awaits!

Entry #2: A Storm Approaches

Dark clouds loom on the horizon. A storm is coming...

The ship creaks under the pressure. The crew braces for impact!

I am the Captain of this Ship!

Rumor #1

"Aye, ye heard about Joyboy? He ain't nothin' special. A boy with a smile and a big mouth. All this 'freedom' talk? Bah! He’s just a fool with a ship and a crew who’s always in danger. They say he’s ‘Captain’, but I reckon it’s just by luck he ain’t dead yet!"

Rumor #2

"Oh, ye best be believin’ the tales of Joyboy! He’s a true free spirit! Never one to waver in his belief that freedom is the most important thing. Smiling through every storm, with a heart that keeps his crew together. That boy’s heart, it’s the size of the sea, and as for his bounty? It don’t matter, he’ll be the Pirate King, mark my words!"
